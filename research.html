Research Topics

Electronic spectroscopy of ions

[Image: Electronic spectroscopy of ions]

This research topic focuses on the study of molecular ions in the gas phase using high-resolution electronic spectroscopy. By cooling ions to cryogenic temperatures, we can probe their electronic structure with unprecedented detail, helping to identify potential carriers of Diffuse Interstellar Bands (DIBs).

Isomer-specific ion-molecule chemistry

[Image: Isomer-specific ion-molecule chemistry]

We investigate the chemical reactivity of specific molecular isomers, particularly those relevant to astrochemical environments. Understanding how different structural arrangements of the same atoms react differently is crucial for modeling the chemical evolution of the interstellar medium.
